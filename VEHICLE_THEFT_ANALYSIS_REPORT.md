# 🚗 Vehicle Theft Analysis Report
**Insights from 500+ Stolen Vehicle Cases (2021-2022)**

---

## 📊 Quick Overview

We analyzed over 500 stolen vehicle cases and discovered some eye-opening patterns about where, when, and what gets stolen. Here's what you need to know.

---

## 🎯 The Main Findings

### 1️⃣ **Trailers Are The Biggest Target**
- **Trailers make up the majority of thefts** - they're by far the most stolen vehicle type
- Why? They're easier to steal, valuable, and often left unattended
- **Action:** If you own a trailer, invest in better security locks and tracking devices

### 2️⃣ **Thieves Have a Color Preference**
| Color | Risk Level |
|-------|-----------|
| 🩶 Silver | HIGHEST |
| ⚪ Grey | HIGHEST |
| ⚪ White | HIGHEST |
| 🔵 Other Colors | Lower |

**What this means:** Silver, grey, and white vehicles are disproportionately targeted. These colors might be easier to resell or blend in better.

### 3️⃣ **Brand New Cars Are At Risk**
- **Vehicles aged 0-5 years** face the highest theft rates
- Newer vehicles have valuable parts and advanced tech that criminals can resell
- Older vehicles (10+ years) are safer from theft (but not from other issues!)

### 4️⃣ **Location, Location, Location**
| Region | Theft Count | Status |
|--------|------------|--------|
| 🔴 Auckland | Highest | HIGH RISK |
| 🔴 Canterbury | High | HIGH RISK |
| ✅ Other Areas | Lower | Safer |

**Why?** Densely populated cities mean more targets, more criminals, and harder for police to monitor.

### 5️⃣ **Thefts Are Getting Worse**
- **2021 to 2022:** Vehicle thefts INCREASED significantly
- The problem is growing, not shrinking
- **Urgent:** We need stronger prevention measures now

---

## 📅 When Do Thefts Happen?

### **Peak Theft Times:**
```
🔴 HIGHEST RISK: March
🟡 WATCH OUT: Mondays & Fridays
⚪ Moderate: Rest of the week
```

**Why Mondays and Fridays?** 
- Monday: Thieves targeting vehicles over the weekend
- Friday: Long weekend opportunities + more foot traffic

### **Seasonal Pattern:**
- March sees the most thefts (spring cleaning season = more people moving/needing vehicles?)
- Plan extra security during March and Fridays

---

## 👥 The Population Connection

**Simple Truth:** 
- **More people = More thieves = More thefts**
- Densely populated areas have significantly higher theft rates
- Rural areas are much safer

## 💡 What You Should Do Right Now

### **If You Own a Vehicle:**
✅ Install a GPS tracker  
✅ Use a steering wheel lock or immobilizer  
✅ Park in well-lit, visible areas  
✅ Don't leave valuables inside  
✅ Consider security cameras  
✅ Install an alarm system  

### **If You Own a Trailer:**
⚠️ This is critical - trailers are the #1 target!  
✅ Use a coupler lock  
✅ Install a wheel clamp  
✅ Mark your trailer with identification  
✅ Keep it in a locked, enclosed space when not in use  
✅ Consider GPS tracking (recommended)  

### **If You Live in Auckland or Canterbury:**
🔴 Be extra vigilant  
✅ Upgrade security measures  
✅ Join neighborhood watch programs  
✅ Report suspicious activity immediately  

### **If You Have a Newer Vehicle (0-5 years old):**
✨ New = Attractive to thieves  
✅ Don't skimp on security  
✅ Park in garages when possible  
✅ Use multiple layers of security
